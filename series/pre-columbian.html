About this series

I was inspired by pre-Columbian pieces from various museums across Colombia — especially the Museo Arqueológico La Merced in Cali and the Museo del Oro in Bogotá. These collections preserve artifacts from the Tumaco, Nariño, San Agustín, Tierradentro, Calima, Tolima, and Quimbaya cultures. This series of drawings translates those ancestral forms — flora, fauna, people, crafts, religion, and death — into a contemporary visual language.
